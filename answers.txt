Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: It helps us easily accomplish distributed version control.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science could be applied to ecology. For example, you could use data science to 
observe the growth/decline in keystone species in an ecosystem and track general biodiversity.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging considers all history in commits, whereas rebasing moves commits to the most
updated version of the branch.
